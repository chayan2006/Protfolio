🎬 The Best Movies According to Angela

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

🐉 Dragon Ball Z

This is my all-time favourite anime 🌟. I love the action, characters, and the stunning visuals that make it unforgettable.

🕷️ Spiderman

Spiderman has always been my hero 🕸️. The story, powers, and action scenes make it one of the best superhero movies ever!

🤖 Iron Man

Iron Man is not just a movie, it’s an emotion 🔥. A perfect blend of science, innovation, and superhero charisma.
